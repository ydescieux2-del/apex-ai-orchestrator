Questions

What people ask before they sign.

How fast can you get a campaign live?
Typically 48 hours from onboarding call to first sends. We need your ICP criteria, a sample of 5-10 ideal prospects, and access to the sending email account. That's it.
Do I need to provide my own lead list?
No. We source leads based on your ICP. If you have an existing list, we'll verify and integrate it — but you don't need anything to get started.
What happens if my domain gets flagged?
Our ZeroBounce verification step removes invalid emails before a single send goes out. We also respect 5-day cooldowns on any contact who's been recently reached. Domain health is built into the system, not bolted on after a problem.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment?
No contract. Month-to-month after setup. Most clients stay because the meetings keep coming — not because they're locked in.
Can I see what's being sent before it goes out?
Yes. You get full sequence approval before activation, plus a live dashboard showing every send, status, and scheduled item in real time.
What industries do you NOT work with?
We don't run campaigns for financial advice, crypto, adult content, or anything requiring regulated disclaimers we can't verify. Everything else is on the table — if you're unsure, just ask on the call.
