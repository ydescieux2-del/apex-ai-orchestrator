Section 01

The Challenge

DataTech Disposition, a Southern California IT asset disposition company, needed to scale outbound lead generation without hiring additional sales staff. Manual prospecting was consuming more than 20 hours per week — time that was pulling the team away from closing deals and servicing existing accounts.

The challenge wasn't finding leads. It was finding the right leads, at scale, with enough personalization to get a response — without a dedicated SDR team to do it.

Section 02

The Solution

Apex AI built a custom Apex AI Orchestrator — an end-to-end AI-powered outreach pipeline purpose-built for the ITAD sector. Five interconnected systems, one seamless workflow.

  1. Automated prospect sourcing across 12 Southern California verticals — finance, healthcare, logistics, manufacturing, and more
  2. ZeroBounce email verification at every stage — 98% deliverability, zero bounce risk, protected sender reputation
  3. AI-personalized outreach sequences — each email references the prospect's vertical, company size, and likely ITAD pain point
  4. Real-time tracking dashboard — pipeline visibility, open rates, click tracking, and response flags in one internal view
  5. Response handling and qualification scoring — inbound replies automatically triaged and scored so sales only touches warm leads
